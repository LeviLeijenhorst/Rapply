# Why Rapply Exists

Rapply exists to eliminate administrative work for reintegration coaches.

Today, coaches:
- Spend hours writing reports
- Reconstruct the same client context repeatedly
- Work across fragmented tools (notes, recordings, documents)
- Struggle with rigid UWV formats

This creates:
- Time loss
- Cognitive overload
- Inconsistent quality
- Frustration

The core problem is NOT writing.
The core problem is lack of structured context.

---

## Key Insight

AI tools like ChatGPT do not solve this.

Why:
- They have no persistent client memory
- They require manual prompting every time
- They are not integrated into workflows
- They do not understand UWV structures

---

## What We Are Building

Rapply is NOT an AI tool.

It is:
A system that manages the full client trajectory and automatically produces compliant outputs.

AI is only used as an invisible layer that:
- structures information
- extracts insights
- generates reports
- answers questions

---

## The Real Goal

A coach should be able to:

- speak after a session  
→ and get a complete report  

- open a client  
→ and instantly understand everything  

- never reconstruct context again  

---

## Core Principle

Understanding context is the product.

Not AI.
Not transcription.
Not writing.

Context.
